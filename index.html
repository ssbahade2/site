About

I am a research scientist and technology entrepreneur specialising in machine learning, computer vision, and AI-driven healthcare. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from Swansea University, where my research focused on deep learning for irregular domains, including 2D and 3D biomedical imaging.

My work spans histopathology, immunostained cancer images, multi-modal microscopy datasets, and geometric deep learning methods such as graph neural networks for cell and nuclei detection and segmentation. I aim to bridge advanced AI research with real clinical and societal impact.

Research

My research interests include medical image analysis, geometric deep learning, graph neural networks, multimodal learning, explainable AI, and large-scale data processing. I have published peer-reviewed work in international journals and conferences and previously worked as a postdoctoral researcher supported by the Wellcome Leap Delta Tissue project.

Startup
