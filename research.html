Research & Publications

Deep Learning for Medical Image Analysis

My research focuses on developing advanced deep learning models for analyzing 2D and 3D biomedical imaging data, including histopathology, immunostained cancer images, and multimodal microscopy datasets. I've published work on graph neural networks for cell and nuclei detection and segmentation, contributing to advances in cancer image analysis.

Geometric Deep Learning

Exploring novel geometric deep learning approaches for irregular domains, with applications in medical imaging and beyond. This includes work on graph neural networks and their applications in analyzing complex biological structures.

Multimodal Learning in Healthcare

Research on integrating multiple data modalities using advanced machine learning techniques to improve diagnostic accuracy and patient outcomes in healthcare applications.
